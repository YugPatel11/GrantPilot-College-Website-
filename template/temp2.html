PIERC: Ideation - Innovation - Incubation

Established in 2013, Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) is a creative hub at Parul University supporting aspiring entrepreneurs. In 2015, PIERC was registered as an incubator to support startups from idea to growth stage. A dedicated team fosters the entrepreneurship ecosystem, helping many founders succeed with their ventures. Learn more.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To create a Startup Support System that enables 5% of students to follow entrepreneurial careers, becoming job creators and contributing to India’s growth.

Mission

  • Foster innovation culture & entrepreneurial spirit through campus initiatives.
  • Create a robust innovation ecosystem to support impactful startups.
  • Develop strong synergy among all stakeholders for end-to-end startup support.
